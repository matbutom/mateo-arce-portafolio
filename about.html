About me :)

My name is Mateo Arce, a graphic designer based in Santiago de Chile and co-founder of Rafita Studio. I have experience in branding, generative art, programming, merchandising, and many other creative things.

Rafita Studio is a space created to share our love for illustration and dogs with the world. We make stickers, prints, shirts and totebags of anything you could imagine related to dogs.
