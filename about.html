About me :)

My name is Mateo Arce, i'm a graphic designer based in Santiago de Chile, co - founder of Rafita Studio, i have experience in branding, generative art, programming, merchandising, and so many others.

Rafita Studio is an space created to express and show the world our love to illustration and dogs, we make stickers, prints, shirts and totebags of anything you could imagine releated to dogs.
